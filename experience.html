An Unforgettable Experience

Before I took SPM in 2017, I used to go on study tours to Petro science and chocolate factories. Well, it is a sweet memory and experience before I finished my school and go further to achieve ambition.

After SPM, I been worked as Facilitator at National Science Center. From December 2017 until 2019. Throughout my service as a facilitator I gained a lot of teaching and experience. I learned to overcome my shyness when i worked as a facilitator, because at the national science center I had to deal with various customer as well as officers and bosses. So, by working there, it helps me to extent my shyness diminished, and I became more confident to speak in public.

I was once involved in a program organized by the national science center which is National Science Week in 2019. Where on the first day I was assigned to be a mascot by carrying a character as a crazy person to promote one of the exhibitions available at the national science center which was an exhibition survive the bunker. It's not simple job or role for me to play a mad character at the moment since I have to sit or lie down in a crowd to attract the visitors,This was the most exciting experience I have ever had.

Next, during my free time, which is in the weekend while I am study, I will work part time with my friend making a Durian Creep. At first, it just for fulfil the time but then the owner called us when we free to work with them. This experience taught me to be patient and good at dividing time between studying as well as working
